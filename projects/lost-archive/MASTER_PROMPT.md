\# Lost Archive — Master Visual Prompt v1.0



\## Purpose



This prompt defines the permanent visual language of the Lost Archive channel.



Every image prompt starts with this block.



Only the scene description changes.



\---



\## Visual Identity



Premium cinematic historical documentary.



BBC Earth meets Netflix documentary.



Ultra photorealistic.



Large-format cinema photography.



Physically accurate lighting.



Natural atmospheric perspective.



Subtle cinematic color grading.



True-to-life textures.



Weathered materials.



No fantasy.



No game aesthetic.



No AI illustration look.



Every frame should feel like a real photograph captured by a documentary crew traveling through time.



\---



\## Historical Style



Historically accurate reconstruction.



Ancient architecture must follow archaeological evidence.



Buildings show centuries of weathering.



Stone surfaces contain erosion.



Edges are imperfect.



Materials feel heavy.



Wood shows age.



Bronze is oxidized.



Marble is slightly worn.



Nothing feels newly built.



Nothing feels decorative for the sake of beauty.



Everything exists because it serves a purpose.



\---



\## Lighting



Natural light only.



Golden sunrise



Warm afternoon



Soft overcast



Blue hour



Real volumetric light.



Never exaggerated god rays.



Never HDR look.



\---



\## Color Palette



Warm limestone



Travertine



Volcanic ash gray



Mediterranean blue



Bronze



Terracotta



Natural vegetation



Muted colors



Never oversaturated.



\---



\## Camera Language



Every prompt must describe the camera.



Examples:



Wide aerial establishing shot.



Slow drone push.



35mm cinema lens.



Natural depth of field.



Foreground → Subject → Background.



Always think like a cinematographer.



\---



\## Composition



Clear focal subject.



Strong foreground.



Layered depth.



Natural leading lines.



Large environmental scale.



Human figures only when needed for scale.



Never overcrowded.



\---



\## Materials



Weathered limestone.



Ancient Roman concrete.



Travertine.



Old timber.



Oxidized bronze.



Sea salt erosion.



Dust.



Mist.



Smoke when appropriate.



Real water physics.



Centuries of weathering.



Sea salt deposits.



Wind erosion.



Uneven masonry.



Patched Roman concrete.



Surface imperfections.



Water stains.



Worn stone steps.



Rope abrasion marks.



Oxidized bronze.



Aged timber.



Evidence of daily commercial activity.



Nothing appears newly built.



\---



\## Human Characters



Historically accurate clothing.



Natural proportions.



Natural poses.



No hero pose.



No cinematic staring.



No smiling at camera.



No AI face artifacts.



\---



\## Documentary Feeling



Every image should answer:



"If a BBC documentary crew had a time machine and photographed this scene, what would it look like?"
