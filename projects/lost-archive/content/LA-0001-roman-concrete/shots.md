## Global Visual Style

Cinematic historical documentary, photorealistic, premium educational
YouTube production, natural lighting, realistic materials, historically
grounded Roman architecture, no fantasy elements, no inaccurate armor,
no text, no subtitles, no logos, no watermark.

Aspect ratio: 16:9\
Frame rate for video: 24 fps\
Clip duration target: 8 seconds\
Camera style: slow, intentional, documentary realism\
Color palette: warm limestone beige, volcanic ash gray, Mediterranean
blue, amber sunrise, cool scientific blue for lab scenes.

Global Negative Prompt: text, watermark, logo, captions, fantasy ruins,
cartoon style, plastic texture, exaggerated CGI, modern construction
helmets in ancient scenes, impossible architecture, low resolution,
blurry image, deformed hands, extra fingers.

    generation.

    the visual bible.

    emotional hold.

    `video_prompts.md`.
