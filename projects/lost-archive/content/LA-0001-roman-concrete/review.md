## Scientific Accuracy QA

### High Confidence Claims

Approved: - Roman marine concrete survives unusually well in seawater. -
Pozzolana, lime, aggregate are core components. - Al-tobermorite and
phillipsite are associated with long-term mineral formation. - Pantheon
remains the largest unreinforced concrete dome. - Modern Portland cement
concrete can degrade in marine conditions.

### Medium Confidence Claims

Use careful wording: - Hot mixing hypothesis. - Lime clasts as
intentional design feature. - CO₂ reduction estimates. - Whether Roman
concrete becomes stronger in all marine contexts.

### Forbidden Claims

Do not say: - Romans intentionally invented self-healing concrete. -
Roman concrete is always better than modern concrete. - Scientists have
fully recreated Roman concrete. - All Roman concrete used one recipe.
