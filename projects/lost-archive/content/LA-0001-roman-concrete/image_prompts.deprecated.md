# Image Prompts --- LA-0001 Roman Concrete

## Global Visual Style

Cinematic historical documentary style, photorealistic, premium
educational YouTube visuals, natural lighting, realistic textures, high
detail, 16:9 aspect ratio, no text, no subtitles, no watermark, no
logos, no modern fantasy elements, historically grounded Roman
architecture and materials.

Color palette: - Warm limestone beige - Volcanic ash gray - Deep
Mediterranean blue - Amber sunrise light - Scientific cool blue for
laboratory and microscopy scenes

Negative prompt for all images: text, watermark, logo, captions,
inaccurate Roman armor, fantasy ruins, cartoon style, plastic texture,
exaggerated CGI, modern construction helmets in ancient scenes,
impossible architecture, low resolution, blurry image, deformed hands,
extra fingers.
